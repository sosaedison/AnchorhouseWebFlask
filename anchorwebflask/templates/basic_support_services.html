Basic Support Services

AnchorHouse LLC offers a range of essential support services to help clients live comfortably and safely in their own homes. These services include 24-hour emergency assistance and adult companion services for personalized care, as well as homemaker services for household support. The company also provides night supervision and respite care, ensuring clients and their families receive the assistance they need when they need it most.

Our Services Include

  • 24-hour emergency assistance
  • Adult companion services
  • Homemaker
  • Individual community living support
  • Individualized home support
  • Night supervision
  • Personal support
  • Respite care, in home or out-of-home
