In-Home Support Services

AnchorHouse's in-home support services are designed to empower clients to lead fulfilling lives in the comfort of their own homes. These services encompass personalized assistance with daily activities, independent living skills training, and support for adults in a semi-independent living setting. With a focus on individualized care, the company ensures each client receives the support they need to maintain their autonomy and well-being.

Our Services Include

  • In-home family support
  • Independent living skills training
  • Individualized home support
  • Individualized home support with family training
  • Individualized home support with training
  • Semi-independent living skills
  • Supported living services for adults
